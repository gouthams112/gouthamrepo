Details of Song:

"Beat It" is a song by American pop icon Michael Jackson, released in 1983 as the third single from his album "Thriller". The song was written and produced by Jackson himself, with co-production from Quincy Jones.

"Beat It" is known for its distinctive guitar riff, performed by Eddie Van Halen, and its memorable music video, directed by Bob Giraldi, which featured a street fight between rival gangs. The song's lyrics describe the need to stand up for oneself and resist peer pressure to join violent conflicts.

"Beat It" was a commercial success, reaching No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and earning Jackson two Grammy Awards for Best Male Rock Vocal Performance and Best Short Form Music Video. The song has since become one of Jackson's most iconic and popular hits, and is often cited as a classic example of his musical style and influence.
