KTM DUKE 390

The KTM 390 is a series of motorcycles manufactured by the Austrian motorcycle manufacturer KTM. The series includes the KTM 390 Duke and the KTM RC 390.

The KTM 390 Duke is a naked bike that features a 373.2cc single-cylinder engine that produces 43 horsepower and 27 Nm of torque. It has a six-speed transmission and a slipper clutch. The bike also features a lightweight steel trellis frame, a WP suspension system, and a high-performance braking system.

The KTM RC 390, on the other hand, is a sports bike that is based on the KTM 390 Duke. It features the same engine and transmission as the Duke, but it has a full-fairing bodywork and a sportier riding position. The bike also has a stiffer suspension system and a larger fuel tank capacity.Both the KTM 390 Duke and the KTM RC 390 come with advanced features such as ride-by-wire throttle, a digital instrument cluster, and LED lighting. They also feature KTM's signature orange color scheme and aggressive styling.

The KTM 390 series is known for its agile handling, strong acceleration, and overall performance. It is a popular choice among riders who are looking for a lightweight, powerful, and versatile motorcycle.
