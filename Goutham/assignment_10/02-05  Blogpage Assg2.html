Hidden placesto visit in goa

There are many hidden and offbeat places in Goa that are worth visiting. Here are some of them:

  • A lush green mountain range located on the Goa-Karnataka-Maharashtra border, known for its scenic beauty and trekking trails.
  • Arambol Sweet Lake: A serene freshwater lake located near Arambol Beach, ideal for swimming, kayaking, and enjoying the natural beauty.
  • Netravali Wildlife Sanctuary: A lesser-known wildlife sanctuary located in the eastern part of Goa, known for its diverse flora and fauna.
  • Harvalem Waterfall: A stunning waterfall located near Sanquelim, hidden amidst lush greenery and rock formations.
  • Galjibaga Beach: A secluded beach located in South Goa, known for its clean white sand and turtle nesting sites.
  • Divar Island: A small and picturesque island located in the Mandovi River, known for its quaint village atmosphere and scenic beauty.
  • Butterfly Beach: A hidden cove located in the Palolem area, accessible only by boat, known for its picturesque beauty and serene environment.
  • Tambdi Surla: A small and ancient temple located in the midst of the forest near the Goa-Karnataka border, known for its unique architecture and history.
